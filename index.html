Our Commitment

The National Health and Education Mission is dedicated to providing world-class services by building a bridge between our clients—volunteers and companies. We work diligently every day to establish bridges of trust and loyalty. Our commitment is to deliver this through personal accountability and professional dedication, collaborating with you to ensure that our services align with your needs. NHEM emphasizes that candidates eager to join the healthcare sector should possess the right vision and a clear understanding of the industry, ensuring a growing career once selected. Our vision is to transform your dreams into reality. We believe in providing a successful career for passionate healthcare enthusiasts, and you can expect excellent services from us.

Our Goals

To be an inspirational company in the healthcare sector by building the best network among our customers – hospitals and volunteers. The healthcare sector is expanding, and the future of healthcare is bright. We will put forth all-around efforts in playing an active role in meeting the high standards of training and manpower services in the healthcare sector. Once recruited, we prepare the candidates to be the best, as healthcare services directly deal with patient safety and time values.
